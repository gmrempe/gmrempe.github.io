A LITTLE ABOUT ME

I am a software engineer from the Rocky Mountains. I went to school at Columbia University and am now in Denver. Programming entered my life while I was doing renewable energy research where I programmed instruments to run experiments. I realized I wanted to move into the tech space and went to a blockchain consulting firm where I recommended blockchain technologies to clients and helped raise funding. From there I moved into full-time web development.

In my free time I enjoy skiing and kayaking, discovering new places to eat and learning new skills. I get excited about seamless apps with great UI/UX, anything involving data and projects that help people achieve. If you have found the world's best cookie or have a cool project I want to talk to you.
